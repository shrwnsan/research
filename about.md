# About Our Research

This is a research blog focused on **agentic systems** and **AI augmentation**. We explore the technical aspects of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and their applications in real-world scenarios.

## Our Focus Areas

- **Agentic AI Systems**: Research on autonomous AI agents and their decision-making processes
- **AI Augmentation**: Studies on how AI can enhance human capabilities
- **Technical Analysis**: Deep dives into AI architectures and implementations
- **Research Methodology**: Best practices for AI research and development

## About the Theme

This blog uses the **Lanyon** theme, a content-first Jekyll theme that prioritizes readability and clean design. The sliding sidebar keeps navigation accessible while maintaining focus on the content.

## Contact

For questions, collaborations, or discussions about our research, please reach out through our GitHub repository.
